Compile/Run Instructions

1) Ensure the folder hierarchy is maintained during extraction
2) Open Terminal/Command Prompt
3) Change directory to main Chomp folder
4) Enter compile code:
javac *.java
5) To run, use the code:
java Driver




Game Instructions

1) You go first:
Select a block of chocolate to chomp
All squares below and to the right will be removed
2) computer selects its piece of chocolate to chomp right after you select your piece.
3)Keep on playing until it asks for "Your turn!" in the console.
4) Whoever is left with the soap piece loses the game and will be declared in the console.
